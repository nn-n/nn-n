SARA
Speech-Assisted Resource Assistant (SARA) is an innovative speech-to-text search bot that introduces a new level of convenience and efficiency to users' digital interactions. With SARA, users can harness the power of their own voice to effortlessly search for information both within their computer and across the internet. This technology enables a seamless integration of natural language and technology, enabling users to simply articulate their queries or topics of interest, eliminating the need for manual typing or intricate navigation.
